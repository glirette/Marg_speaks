# Case Status Refresh - 2026-06-08

This update records the current public status of the case covered by the original `default.htm` page.

The original GitHub page described itself as a living blog following the justice process of Terry Speaks and Margaret A. Sanchez. GitHub was one experimental publishing channel, not the final live blog system. Greg Lirette continued following the case through other channels, but this repository was not kept current at the time.

This note refreshes the repository without changing the original artifact.

## Status Summary

### Terry Christopher Speaks

Public court-record source:

- In `State of Louisiana v. Terry Speaks`, No. 16-KA-163, the Louisiana Fifth Circuit Court of Appeal states that Terry Speaks appealed his convictions and sentences from the 24th Judicial District Court, Jefferson Parish.
- The opinion states that Speaks was indicted for second degree murder of Jaren Lockhart, obstruction of justice, and conspiracy to obstruct justice.
- The opinion states that the jury found Speaks guilty as charged on all counts.
- The opinion states that the trial court sentenced him to life imprisonment at hard labor without benefit of parole, probation, or suspension of sentence on count one, plus additional sentences on the obstruction-related counts.
- The opinion affirmed the convictions and sentences.

Public news source:

- WDSU reported that Terry Speaks was sentenced to life without parole in the murder of Jaren Lockhart.

### Margaret A. Sanchez

Public news source:

- WDSU reported that Margaret Sanchez pleaded guilty in Jaren Lockhart's death and dismemberment.
- WDSU's article description states that Sanchez was sentenced to 40 years in prison.
