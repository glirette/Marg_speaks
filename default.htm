Oct 15th update:

Today 5 significant things took place in Jefferson Parish related to this case.

 

1)      As scheduled MARGARET A SANCHEZ appeared in Division J early in the morning. She was informed that her next court appearance if a motions hearing prior to trial and is scheduled for November 17, 2014 at 9:00AM. Trial is documented to be the week of 01/12/2015. This is new information as of today.

 

         2)   Terry Speaks was scheduled for an Arraignment but this is almost as if it were a placeholder because he was in federal custody. Terry Speaks was brought to Jefferson Parish today and booked. The Jefferson Parish website lists the charges as follows:

MURDER SECOND DEGREE

OBSTRUCTION OF JUSTICE

REGISTRATION OF SEX OFFENDERS

 

Terry Speaks has two separate cases one dealing with the murder and the other dealing with sex offender registration.

 

I would expected there to be a charge added to the site soon for conspiracy obstruction of justice just as it is for Margaret A Sanchez.

Terry Speaks bail amount is currently set to $0 on all meaning no bond but that is sure to change after he is Arraignment.

MARGARET A SANCHEZ has already plead not guilty and is clearly proceeding to trial. Since Speaks was just now booked and has not even had his arraignment yet it would appear that his trail may happen later.

2)      Prosecution announced they intended to severe the defendants. This is probably because one will cooperate.

 

       4)  Discovery has been provided to the defense (both attorneys)

 

It is as follows:

 

1. 8 page autopsy protocol

2. 1 page toxicology report

3. 1 page document from Handcock County Medical Examiner's Office

4. 1 page State of Mississippi permit for Cremation or Burial at Sea

This was hand delivered to both attorneys on Oct 15th, 2014

 

5) Terry Speaks arraignment was reset for Monday October 20th, 2014 because he is in transit to JPCC.

The arrestee's vehicle was documented in Kenner, LA on a route toward a home that Sanchez and Speaks shared in Kenner, LA, A short time after Sanchez, Speaks and the victim were last seen walking together.. The arrestee's vehicle was also documented entering the state of MISSISSIPPI and exiting the state of MISSISSIPPI during a time period after the victim left with Sanchez and Speaks and before the victim's body parts washed ashore. The body was dismembered and swatches of skin which bore different tattoos were strategically removed from the victims body in an effort to hinder identification and thwart any criminal investigation.
